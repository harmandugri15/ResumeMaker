## Quantification When You Don't Have Exact Numbers

Sometimes you lack precise metrics. Use these strategies:

45. **Estimate conservatively**: "Managed approximately 30 client accounts"
46. **Use ranges**: "Processed 200–300 applications per week"
47. **Use frequency**: "Delivered executive presentations on a bi-weekly basis to C-suite stakeholders"
48. **Use scale**: "Supported an organization of 5,000+ employees"
49. **Use comparisons**: "Ranked #3 out of 50 sales representatives regionally"
50. **Use before/after**: "Reduced onboarding time from 3 weeks to 5 days"
51. **Use scope**: "Led initiative spanning 4 countries and 12 teams"
52. **Use percentages**: "Improved process efficiency by approximately 25%"

The key: even approximate numbers are far more powerful than no numbers at all.
