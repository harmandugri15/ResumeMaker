# Resume Tips for Technology Roles (Software Engineering, Data Science, IT, Product Management)

## General Guidelines
*   **Focus on Impact, Not Just Tools:** Don't just list technologies. Explain what you built with them and the resulting business impact (e.g., "Built a REST API using Node.js that processed 10,000 requests/sec, reducing latency by 40%").
*   **Keywords are Crucial:** ATS systems for tech roles heavily filter based on specific languages, frameworks, and methodologies (e.g., Agile, CI/CD, React, Python). Ensure these appear in context, not just in a list.
*   **Link to Portfolio:** Always include links to your GitHub, personal website, or specific projects. Code quality matters.
*   **Keep it Relevant:** If you're a senior engineer, don't list basic skills like "Microsoft Word" or HTML/CSS unless applying for a frontend-heavy role.
*   **Show Continuous Learning:** Tech evolves rapidly. Mention recent courses, certifications (e.g., AWS Certified Solutions Architect), or personal projects using new tech.

## Software Engineering
*   **Highlight Architecture & Design:** Mention if you designed system architecture, database schemas, or API contracts.
*   **Quantify Scale:** Use numbers to describe scale (e.g., users, data volume, request rate, availability percentage).
*   **Testing & CI/CD:** Highlight your experience with automated testing (unit, integration) and deployment pipelines.

## Data Science & Analytics
*   **Business Outcomes:** Connect your models to business results (e.g., "Developed a churn prediction model that reduced customer attrition by 15%, saving $2M annually").
*   **Data Pipeline:** Mention your role in data extraction, cleaning, and pipeline creation, not just modeling.
*   **Tool Stack:** Clearly list libraries and tools (Pandas, Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch, SQL, Tableau).

## Product Management
*   **User-Centric Metrics:** Focus on metrics like Monthly Active Users (MAU), Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), retention rate, and conversion rate.
*   **Cross-Functional Leadership:** Emphasize your ability to lead teams without direct authority (engineers, designers, marketing).
*   **Product Lifecycle:** Detail your experience taking a product from ideation to launch and post-launch iteration.

## IT & DevOps
*   **Uptime & Reliability:** Highlight improvements in system uptime (e.g., "Achieved 99.99% uptime") and reductions in downtime.
*   **Automation:** Focus on processes you automated (e.g., "Automated server provisioning using Terraform, reducing setup time by 80%").
*   **Security:** Mention any security protocols implemented or audits passed.

## Essential Action Verbs
Architected, Deployed, Engineered, Refactored, Optimized, Scaled, Automated, Built, Integrated, Modeled, Analyzed, Formulated, Launched, Spearheaded, Monitored.
