# Resume Tips for Sales & Business Development Roles

## General Guidelines
*   **Numbers Speak Louder Than Words:** A sales resume without hard numbers is a red flag. Quantify everything: quota attainment, revenue generated, deal sizes, territory size, and growth percentages.
*   **Highlight the "How":** Don't just say you hit a number; briefly mention *how* (e.g., "Exceeded quota by 120% through aggressive outbound prospecting and expansion of key accounts").
*   **Awards and Recognition:** Prominently feature President's Club, Rep of the Year, or consistent top-performer rankings (e.g., "Ranked #1 out of 50 reps nationwide").
*   **Sales Methodologies:** List specific methodologies you are trained in (e.g., MEDDIC, Challenger Sale, SPIN, BANT).

## Account Executives & Direct Sales
*   **Quota Performance:** State your quota and your attainment percentage clearly for each role (e.g., "Achieved 135% of $1.5M annual quota").
*   **Deal Metrics:** Mention your Average Contract Value (ACV), sales cycle length, and win rate.
*   **Target Audience:** Specify who you sold to (e.g., "Sold Enterprise SaaS solutions to C-level executives in the Fortune 500").

## Business Development & SDRs/BDRs
*   **Activity Metrics:** Highlight your daily/weekly activity levels (e.g., "Averaged 80 cold calls and 50 personalized emails daily").
*   **Pipeline Generation:** Focus on the pipeline you created, not just closed won (e.g., "Generated $2.5M in qualified pipeline in Q3, resulting in $800K in closed revenue").
*   **Conversion Rates:** Mention your meeting-set-to-held ratio or SQL (Sales Qualified Lead) generation rate.

## Sales Management & Leadership
*   **Team Performance:** Focus on the performance of the team you managed (e.g., "Led a team of 8 AEs to achieve 110% of a $10M team quota").
*   **Hiring & Coaching:** Highlight your experience recruiting top talent and reducing ramp time for new hires.
*   **Process Improvement:** Detail how you improved the sales process, implemented new CRM workflows, or refined the go-to-market strategy.

## Essential Action Verbs
Closed, Exceeded, Generated, Negotiated, Prospected, Secured, Upsold, Expanded, Acquired, Penetrated, Forecasted, Pitched.
