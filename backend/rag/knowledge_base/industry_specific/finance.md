# Resume Tips for Finance & Accounting Roles

## General Guidelines
*   **Precision is Paramount:** Finance resumes must be 100% error-free. A single typo or calculation error can be a dealbreaker.
*   **Quantify Everything:** Use absolute numbers, percentages, and dollar amounts (e.g., "Managed a $50M portfolio," "Reduced expenses by 15% ($200K)").
*   **Format Matters:** Use a conservative, traditional layout (like the Executive Pro template). Avoid flashy graphics or colors.
*   **Highlight Certifications:** Certifications (CPA, CFA, CFP) are critical. Put them after your name (e.g., John Doe, CFA) and prominently in the Education or Certifications section.

## Investment Banking & Private Equity
*   **Deal Experience:** Detail your role in specific transactions (M&A, IPOs, debt issuance), including deal size and outcome.
*   **Financial Modeling:** Specifically mention the types of models you built (LBO, DCF, three-statement).
*   **Action Verbs:** Use strong verbs like "Executed," "Originated," "Structured," "Analyzed."

## Accounting & Audit
*   **Regulatory Compliance:** Highlight your knowledge of relevant standards (GAAP, IFRS, SOX, SEC reporting).
*   **Efficiency Improvements:** Focus on process improvements, such as shortening the month-end close process or automating reconciliations.
*   **Audit Outcomes:** Mention successful audits, identifying control weaknesses, or saving money through tax optimization.

## Corporate Finance & FP&A
*   **Variance Analysis:** Describe your experience analyzing budget vs. actuals and providing strategic insights to management.
*   **Forecasting & Budgeting:** Highlight your role in the annual planning cycle and long-term financial modeling.
*   **Cross-Functional Collaboration:** Mention how you partnered with other departments (sales, marketing, ops) to drive financial performance.

## Financial Advisory & Wealth Management
*   **Client Relationship Management:** Focus on client acquisition, retention, and assets under management (AUM) growth.
*   **Portfolio Performance:** If applicable, state how your portfolio recommendations performed relative to benchmarks.
*   **Compliance:** Emphasize adherence to FINRA/SEC regulations.

## Essential Action Verbs
Allocated, Audited, Forecasted, Maximized, Reconciled, Underwrote, Yielded, Structured, Modeled, Appraised, Liquidated, Consolidated.
