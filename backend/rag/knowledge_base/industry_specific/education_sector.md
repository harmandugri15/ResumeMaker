# Resume Tips for the Education Sector (Teaching, Administration, Research)

## General Guidelines
*   **Certifications are Key:** State your teaching licenses, certifications, and endorsements clearly at the top of the resume.
*   **Focus on Student Outcomes:** Don't just list your duties. Show how you impacted students (e.g., "Increased state test scores in math by 15%," "Improved student attendance by 10%").
*   **Adaptability:** Highlight your ability to adapt to different learning styles, special needs (IEPs/504s), and remote/hybrid learning environments.
*   **Format:** Education resumes can sometimes be longer than the standard 1-page corporate resume, especially for academics, but keep K-12 teaching resumes concise and focused.

## K-12 Teaching
*   **Classroom Management:** Describe specific strategies or programs you use for behavior management and creating a positive learning environment.
*   **Curriculum Development:** Highlight experience designing lesson plans, aligning curriculum with state/national standards, and integrating technology.
*   **Extracurricular Involvement:** Mention if you sponsor clubs, coach sports, or serve on school committees, as this shows dedication to the school community.
*   **Parent Communication:** Emphasize how you maintain proactive communication with parents and guardians.

## Higher Education & Academia
*   **The CV vs. Resume:** Understand the difference. A CV (Curriculum Vitae) is often expected and should include comprehensive lists of publications, presentations, grants, and courses taught.
*   **Research Focus:** Clearly state your research interests and highlight significant grants secured or papers published in peer-reviewed journals.
*   **Service:** Mention service to the university (committee work) and the profession (reviewing papers, organizing conferences).

## Educational Administration (Principals, Superintendents)
*   **Leadership & Vision:** Focus on school-wide or district-wide initiatives you led and their impact on performance or culture.
*   **Budget Management:** Quantify the size of the budgets you managed and any cost-saving measures implemented.
*   **Staff Development:** Highlight experience hiring, evaluating, and mentoring teachers and staff.

## Essential Action Verbs
Educated, Instructed, Mentored, Facilitated, Assessed, Curated, Developed (Curriculum), Fostered, Guided, Motivated, Published, Secured (Grants).
