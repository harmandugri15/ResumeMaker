## Usage Tips

1. **Match the verb to the impact level.** Use "spearheaded" or "pioneered" for initiatives you owned end-to-end. Use "contributed to" or "supported" only when you played a smaller role.
2. **Avoid repeating the same verb** across consecutive bullets. Vary your language.
3. **Use present tense** for current roles, **past tense** for previous roles.
4. **Pair the verb with a result**: "Optimized" alone means nothing — "Optimized CI/CD pipeline, reducing deployment time by 60%" is compelling.
